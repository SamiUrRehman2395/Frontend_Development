Good Overall Facewash - Don't expect too much thought

I am impressed at Dari Mooch distribution system, as this product is available at most big grocery stores, I got mine from one of these stores.
As far as a Facewash, i believe its a very good one, it lathers well and a little amount of it works very well. It does leave your face slightly dry, which is not a problem for me - atleast in the summers, as i have oily skin. However with people with dry skin, it may create slight discomfort, but nothing bad. It cleans the face of the dirt and prevents oil build up therefore reducing acne. As a Facewash its 10/10

Now for the advertised "De-tan" properties, I've been using this for a month and I definitely see a difference, however its not a huge one. I have a tan from frequent travelling, and it did help in evening out my face's skintone (and that too very subtle) but it by no means made me any more fairer than i was in the past. You can expect it to work, but don't expect a night and day difference. A tan is not only present on your face but other areas too, therefore couple it with the de tan body wash (i have not tried it out, I'll get it soon and leave a review as well)

Overall it is a good de tan facewash, I will continue using it. However it is important to note that this facewash is by no means a replacement for your sunscreen, the effectiveness of this only relies on your consistent use of sunscreen. Which in general is always recommended to be used whenever going outside.
